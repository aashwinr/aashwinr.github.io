Raycaster

This is a 2D raycaster that uses DDA for fast intersection calculations.
This is one of my earlier projects. I previously used SFML with C++ for graphics, but this is a ported version that uses canvas.

Guide:

  • The box on the left is the map.
  • The box on the right is the "3D" view.
  • Place some walls by clicking/dragging on the map.


Controls:

  • W: Move forward
  • A: Turn left
  • S: Move back
  • D: Turn right
  • Click on map: Place wall
